Overflow Property

This is a div with a fixed width and height. The content is longer than the container, and `overflow: hidden` will hide the excess text. This demonstrates the `overflow-x` and `overflow-y` properties implicitly, as setting a fixed size can lead to content exceeding the boundaries.

This is another div with a fixed size. The content here is also longer than the container, but `overflow: scroll` will add scrollbars to let you view all of the content. This is useful when you have a large amount of text that you don't want to overflow the page layout.
